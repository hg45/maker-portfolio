Hands study

I drew my drawing hand as it drew, so I didn't have to look back and forth between my artwork and my reference.

3 Value Viewfinder Sketch

I did a 3 value sketch of various items, such as my keychain and an outlet, surrounded by my scrunched up sweater

Remixed Value Drawing

For this we did several 20-minute figure drawings of various student models, and then picked one that we liked and remixed it with mixed-media elements. In my case I made heavy use of collage.

Paper Cup Sculpture Challenge

For this challenge, we were given 2 or 3 paper cups and tasked with transforming them into a sculpture that was unrecognizable as the original paper cups. I quite liked mine and I think it was the only one that's bouncy due to the way that its supports are designed.

Pop Art Print

We had a mini unit on print making and pop art, and I (a non-christian) took inspiration from Jesus and made him buff using a reference of 4 time Olympia winner Chris Bumstead, which allowed me to title it with the pun Christ Bumstead.

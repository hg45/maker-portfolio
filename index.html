Who am I?

I am Henry Grace, a Class of 2026 student at Stuyvesant Highschool. I am an amateur artist but I do what I can. I have some experience with front-end website building (hence the aesthetic of this website, which is based on my personal universal CSS file that applies to every website my browser visits using an extension). I am a student athlete for the PSAL Boys Gymnastics Team, and won Rookie of the Year as a Freshman. Outside of school I do rock climbing. When Gymnastics is not in season I work in Stuyvesant Theater Community productions for various crews. I am genderqueer, bisexual, and go by any pronouns, and my queerness oftentimes influences my artwork. Other influences include my religion and my interests in mathematics and linguistics. In my doodles, one would typically find pentagrams or the sitelen pona glyph for the Toki Pona word kijetesantakalu.
